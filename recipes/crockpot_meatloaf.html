Crock Pot Meatloaf

That's a tasty-looking meatloaf!

This is perhaps the best meatloaf I've ever had! The crock pot allows the meatloaf to cook over the course of several hours without drying out.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs 80/20 ground beef
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 box (6 oz) dried stuffing mix
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Brown sugar/ketchup glaze
    • 3/4 cup of ketchup
    • 1/8 cup of brown sugar
    • 1 tablespoon finely minced onion
    • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Dice onion finely
  2. Mix all brown sugar/ketchup glaze ingredients and set aside
  3. Line crock pot with foil and spray lightly with cooking spray
  4. Mix ground beef, the rest of that onion, stuffing mix, eggs, salt, pepper, milk, and 2 tablespoons of glaze together with your hands
  5. Shape into a reasonably loaf-like shape and place in crock pot
  6. Top with 1/2 cup of glaze
  7. Cook on Low for 6 hours
  8. Lift the meatloaf out of the crock pot by the foil
  9. Slice and serve with mashed potatoes, or mac and cheese. Vegetables not recommended.
