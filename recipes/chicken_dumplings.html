Crock Pot Chicken and Dumplings

Above: Chicken and dumplings.

This is a delicious and simple version of chicken and dumplings that can be made in the crock pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 large Onion
  • 1 can Cream of Celery soup
  • 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 4 thawed chicken breasts
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups thawed (formerly frozen) peas and carrots blend
  • 1 can refrigerated biscuits (buttermilk, country, home style, or southern)
  • 1 tablespoon of some sort of chicken seasoning. I used sage and Trader Joe's Green Goddess
  • Fresh parsley / rosemary if itâ€™s around (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut onion and place in crock pot
  2. Place chicken breasts on top of the onions
  3. Combine the soups and spices in a bowl, then spread over chicken
  4. Pour chicken broth in to the crock pot
  5. Add sprig of rosemary if desired
  6. Cook on High for 4 hours
  7. Roll each biscuit flat and cut into squares
  8. Add peas and carrots to crock pot and stir
  9. Throw diced biscuits on top (do this all quickly, getting the lid back on ASAP)
  10. Continue cooking on High for another hour, make sure biscuits are reasonably cooked
  11. Shred the chicken
  12. Add parsley if desired
  13. Cook 10 more minutes
  14. Serve in a bowl
