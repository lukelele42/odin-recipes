Sloppy Steaks

Above: correct steak hydration technique

Popularized by Tim Robinson on the Netflix Original Series I Think You Should Leave, Sloppy Steaks have grown into a world-wide phenomenon. With this tried and true recipe, you too can be a real piece of shit, making your very own Sloppy Steaks in either the comfort of your kitchen or in your favorite steakhouse!

It makes the night so much more fun!

Ingredients

  • Large, rare cut of steak (preferably a T-bone)
  • 16 oz of ice water

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron skillet on high for five minutes
  2. Sear steak for approximately 20 seconds on each side
  3. Remove steak from skillet and place on plate to rest- the steak should be rare
  4. While steak is resting, fill a pint glass with ice and water
  5. Finally, take the glass of ice water and dump it all over the steak! Really splash it around!

If making this recipe at home, simply sit down and enjoy your homemade Sloppy Steak!

If you decided to undertake these steps in a nice steakhouse, the waiters will come running over to snatch it up, so you gotta eat fast as you can!

Remember, they'll say "No Sloppy Steaks!", but they can't stop you from ordering a steak and a glass of water!

It makes the night so much more fun!
